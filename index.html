Sync Across Devices

Export your data to use on other devices

How to Sync Across Devices:

  1. On your current device, click "Export Data" to download your database file
  2. Transfer the downloaded file to your other device (email, cloud storage, USB, etc.)
  3. On the other device, open this website and click "Import Data"
  4. Select the database file you transferred
  5. Your data will now be synchronized across both devices!
